Gameplay

This is a very straightforward simulation game devised by Mathematician John Conway in 1970.

  • You click a few cells to start off, effectively bringing them to "life".
  • Any living cell that has 2 or 3 adjacent living cells will live on. Otherwise they "die".
  • If a "dead" cell is surrounded by exactly 3 "living" cells, the dead cell comes to life.
